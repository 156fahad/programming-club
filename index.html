About Us

Programming Club is a platform where students with different interests such as, coding, App development, Machine Learning, and Web Designing. The Coding Club provides participating students an opportunity to learn the basics of computer programming in a team setting. The club's main focus is on creating applications, websites, games, stem/steam workshops, and other projects to share with LCC and the community. Networking and referrals: Coding Clubs work as a network where you can interact and work with students from other colleges too. Being a part of such a club allows the alumni team from big companies to refer your resume, which helps you get over one of the biggest hurdles of college placements..
